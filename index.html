Always document your code!

  • Top of the function - document your code!
  • Variable with weird naming - document your code!
  • Middle of a function that looks tricky to understand. - document your code for the next person!

People always talk about simplifying their system to make it run faster and smoother but yet majority of the application isn't simplified enough.

Slow is fast and fast is slow. A well documented system makes for finding the issue and correcting the issue fast and effective.
